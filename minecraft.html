Welcome to the Minecraft Page!

The new update for minecraft, Java Edition 1.14, comes out April 23rd 2019 and will bring a ton of new items to the game including Bamboo, Pillagers, and much more! The new update is called the "Village & Pillage" update and brings new mobs called Pillagers which are similar to Villagers but instead of trading with you, Pillagers invade villages and pillage them. They ride on another new mob called the "Pillager's Beast". The Beast will invade a village with the Pillagers and trample the Villager's crops but won't destroy blocks. When the Pillagers invade villages they carry a new weapon called the Crossbow. The crossbow is very similar to the Bow, you need to pull the arrow back and press right click again to shoot out an arrow. You can also shoot fireworks out of the Crossbow.
