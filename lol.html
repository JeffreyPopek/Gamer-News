Welcome to the League of Legends Page!

League of legends, or Riot Games, has said that they will release new skins in the upcoming patch 9.8. Riot has said they are releasing 8 news skins in patch 9.8, these skins are IG (Invictus Gaming) Camille, IG Fiora, IG Irelia, IG Kai'Sa, IG LeBlanc, IG Rakan, Galaxy Slayer Zed, and Prestige Edition KD/A Evelynn. You can buy the IG skins for a limited time in the shop for probaly about 1350 RP a skin. Galaxy Slayer Zed is a legendary skins and will be priced at 1820 RP. Finally Prestige Edition KD/A Evelynn will cost 100 prestige points and can be bought for about a year after release. Currently there is only one way to get prestige points which is buying bundles in the shop and will only give you about 10 points and will cost you 2250 RP (it also includes 10 masterwork chests and keys).

The new patch 9.8 is coming out on 9/17/19 and it comes with all the skins metioned above and nerfs and buffs to champions and items. For more information on the patches go to the the League of Legends website to read in detail all the changes to each character that is being buffed or nerfed.
