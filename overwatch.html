Welcome to the Overwatch page!

The newest event in Overwatch is the new Storm Rising Event. This event will be in the new map Havana, Cuba. In this mission you play as either Tracer, Winstion, Genji, or Mercy and your goal is to capture a Omnic Talon Agent and obtain valuble information from the omnic. This event will last from April 16th to May 6th.
